Everything a Pro Needs

Flowstate Audio delivers the full Logic Pro experience — modeled on its workflow, feature-for-feature, in a modern standalone app.

🎙
Multi-Track Recording
Unlimited audio and MIDI tracks. Punch recording, take folders, comping — the full Logic Pro recording workflow.
🔌
VST3 / AU Plugins
Load your own plugins. Built-in: 8-band EQ, compressor, reverb, delay, limiter, chorus, distortion, channel EQ.
🎹
Piano Roll
Full MIDI editor with velocity editing, quantize (1/4 to 1/32 + triplets), chord detection, Hyper Draw automation.
🎚
Pro Mixer
Per-channel inserts (4 slots), sends, pan, VU meters, groups — a full mixing console like Logic's.
🤖
Clawbot AI
Generate beats, melodies, full tracks via Suno & MusicGen. AI mastering, stem separation, arrangement suggestions.
⏱
Flex Time & Pitch
Non-destructive time stretching and pitch shifting. Quantize audio to grid without artifacts.
🎼
Score Editor
Real-time MIDI-to-notation. Print or export sheet music. Part box, lyrics, chord grids, tablature.
🔄
Automation
Per-track automation lanes for volume, pan, EQ, and plugin parameters. Draw or record automation in real time.
📦
Stem Export
Bounce entire project or individual stems. WAV (16/24/32-bit float), MP3, AIFF, LUFS normalisation.
🥁
Step Sequencer
Pattern-based beat programming. Per-step velocity, pitch, probability. Up to 32 steps per pattern.
🔁
Loop Recording
Record in loop mode — each pass creates a new take. Comp your best moments just like Logic Pro's take folders.
🌐
Flowstate Connected
Clawbot connects directly to your Flowstate Hub account. Coin system, subscription, and AI usage all unified.
